The Best Movies According to Erick

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Peaky Blinders

When Thomas Shelby sets his sights on a goal, nothing and no one can deter him.

Vikings

As he rises to power, Ragnar embodies the essence of a Viking leader. Yet, his personal ambitions and love for his family, especially Lagertha and Aslaug, often conflict, leading to internal and external battles.

Queen of The South

Teresa Mendoza's rise from poverty to power within the drug trade. Showcasing her resilience and intellect in a male-dominated world, it's an empowering tale of survival and strategy.
